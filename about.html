About TestRedirect

We're on a mission to make URL redirect testing simple, fast, and free for everyone. Built by developers, for developers.

Our Story

TestRedirect was born out of frustration. As web developers and SEO professionals, we constantly needed to test URL redirects during site migrations, HTTPS implementations, and SEO audits. Existing tools were either too expensive, too slow, or too complicated.

So we built TestRedirect – a fast, free, and straightforward redirect checker that does exactly what you need. No signups, no paywalls, no unnecessary features. Just enter a URL and get instant results.

Since launch, we've helped thousands of webmasters, developers, and SEO professionals test millions of URLs. And we're just getting started.

What We Offer

TestRedirect provides comprehensive redirect analysis tools:

  • Single URL Checker: Instantly analyze any URL's redirect chain with detailed status codes and path visualization.
  • Bulk Redirect Checker: Test up to 1000 URLs at once – perfect for site migrations and comprehensive audits.
  • User Agent Testing: See how different browsers and crawlers (including Googlebot) experience your redirects.
  • CSV Export: Download results for easy reporting and analysis.
  • HTTPS Verification: Ensure your redirects properly route to secure URLs.
